You can use the reports section of cockpit to display custom statistics and reports. Please note that this section is not visible if no report is available. Refer to the [Cockpit Plugins]({{< ref "/manual/webapps/cockpit/extend/plugins.md" >}}) section for adding a custom reporting plugin.
